## 💡 Strategy & Optimization Tactics

### 1. **Audience Segmentation**
- Targeted high-intent audiences by geography, demographics, and behavior  
- A/B tested audience clusters for optimal performance  

### 2. **Campaign Optimization**
- Managed CPC & CPM to maximize lead volume and efficiency  
- Tested multiple creatives and copy for CTR improvement  
- Reallocated budget to top-performing ad sets  

### 3. **Analytics-Driven Decisions**
- SQL dashboards tracked daily KPIs: CTR, CPC, CPM, Lead Quality Score  
- Python scripts automated reporting and trend analysis  
- Multi-market attribution analysis identified high-value audiences  

### 4. **Conversion Funnel Optimization**
- Pixel tracking ensured accurate revenue attribution  
- Retargeting high-intent leads improved ROI  
- Focused on conversions ($600 FTDs), not just clicks
