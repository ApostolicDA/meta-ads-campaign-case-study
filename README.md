## üí° Strategy & Optimization Tactics

### 1. **Audience Segmentation**
- Targeted high-intent audiences in specific geographic markets
- Used demographic and behavioral targeting to reduce CAC
- A/B tested audience clusters for optimal performance

### 2. **Campaign Optimization**
- **CPC Optimization:** Managed cost-per-click to maximize lead volume
- **CPM Management:** Optimized impressions-to-lead ratio
- **CTR Improvement:** Tested multiple ad creatives and copy variations
- **Budget Allocation:** Reallocated spend to highest-performing ad sets

### 3. **Analytics-Driven Decisions**
- Built SQL-based reporting dashboard to track daily performance
- Monitored key metrics: CTR, CPC, CPM, Lead Quality Score
- Used trend analysis to identify optimization opportunities
- A/B tested landing pages to improve conversion rates

### 4. **Conversion Funnel Optimization**
- Optimized pixel tracking for accurate attribution
- Monitored lead quality metrics beyond just volume
- Tracked FTD conversion to measure true business impact
- Implemented retargeting for high-intent audiences
